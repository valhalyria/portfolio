Who am I?

I am Melanie, born in 1993 in Belgium. I graduated in English and German linguistics, as well as in multilingual communication. In 2020, I moved permanently to Berlin, Germany.
I have always been curious, eager to learn new skills and improve myself on different levels. Being the daughter of a self-taught computer nerd and technology enthusiast, it was only natural for me to dabble in programming languages. I have looked into Kotlin a bit before joining SheCodes coding workshops in 2022. I am currently perfecting my skills in HTML, CSS and Javascript. I completed several SheCodes workshop and am currently enrolled in the React workshop.
